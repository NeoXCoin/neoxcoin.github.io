How NeoX Works

NeoX (NXC) is a decentralized token powered by smart contracts on the Ethereum network (Sepolia for testing).

NeoX enables seamless participation in digital economies, allowing users to buy, hold, and interact with NFTs, DeFi, and future Web3 applications.

The token is launched using ERC-20 standards and is publicly accessible through a secure minting platform.

NeoX aims to evolve into a utility or governance token that supports the NeoX NFT ecosystem and future blockchain features.
