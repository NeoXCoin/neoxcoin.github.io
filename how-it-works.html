How it Works

The core of NeoX’s design is practicality, security, and decentralization.

NeoX is built on the ERC-20 standard, ensuring high-speed, stable transactions and compatibility with popular wallets such as MetaMask. Transparency through smart contracts and community governance ensures the value integrity of Web3.

Beyond being a cryptocurrency, NeoX also provides foundational NFT infrastructure, allowing users to mint, trade, and hold NFTs — building a unique ecosystem of digital assets.

By participating in NeoX, you're not just investing once — you're joining a movement that unlocks an innovative, trusted future of digital finance.
