How it Works

NeoX operates as a decentralized digital currency built on the Ethereum blockchain using the ERC-20 standard. It is designed for speed, security, and compatibility with popular wallets like MetaMask.

Every token transaction is recorded on-chain, ensuring transparency and immutability. Users can easily connect to dApps, trade tokens, or interact with smart contracts using their preferred Web3 wallet.

NeoX also integrates with a multi-generation NFT ecosystem, offering holders access to exclusive content, governance features, and evolving utilities.

As the ecosystem expands, new tools and community-driven innovations will enhance the NeoX experience across DeFi, gaming, identity, and more.
