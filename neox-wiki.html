Overview

NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem built on Base Mainnet, combining digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse-style experiences under a unified brand. The ecosystem includes the NeoX Coin (NXC), curated NFT series, an HTML5 arcade game, and AI-enhanced multimedia projects.

History

NeoX originated as a creative digital art project and gradually expanded to incorporate blockchain technology, NFTs, and interactive media. In 2025, the NeoX Coin (NXC) was launched on Base Mainnet, marking NeoX’s transition into a broader on-chain ecosystem spanning collectibles, gaming, and metaverse experimentation.

NeoX Coin (NXC)

NXC is the utility token of the NeoX ecosystem and is deployed on Base Mainnet. It is designed to support participation across NeoX experiences and future metaverse features.

NFT Collections

NeoX features several curated NFT series, including:

  • NeoX Timekeeper Edition – 8 limited NFTs themed around time and perception.
  • NeoX Van Gogh Visions – Artworks inspired by Van Gogh’s style blended with NeoX aesthetics.
  • NeoX Abstract Realms – A conceptual abstract art collection.
  • NeoCoin Genesis Edition (G1) – An early foundational art series for NeoX.

NeoX Catch Game

The NeoX Catch Game is a browser-based HTML5 arcade game where players collect coins, avoid obstacles, and progress through multiple stages.

Key features include:

  • Multi-level progression with an opening sequence and end scene
  • Score tracking, including Top 3 leaderboard display
  • Mobile-first responsive layout for phones and tablets
  • Visual integration with the NeoX brand identity

Metaverse Exhibition

NeoX maintains a virtual exhibition environment using platforms such as OnCyber, featuring NFT artworks, 3D avatar integration, ambient media, and cinematic scenes. The exhibition is updated as new collections and experimental spaces are introduced.

Technology Stack

NeoX makes use of the following technologies:

  • Base Mainnet (L2) for token and contract deployment
  • Thirdweb for contract deployment and management
  • IPFS / Lighthouse for decentralized storage
  • HTML5 & Canvas for game implementation
  • VRM / glTF assets for avatars and metaverse visuals
  • Responsive design optimized for mobile and iOS devices

Future Development

Planned developments include extended metaverse spaces, new NFT collections, AI-assisted micro-films, upgraded game mechanics, and deeper cross-platform integrations across web, mobile, and virtual worlds.
