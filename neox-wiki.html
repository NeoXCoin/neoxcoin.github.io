NeoX (NXC) – Ecosystem Overview

BASE MAINNET ERC-20 TOKEN NFT COLLECTIONS GAME METAVERSE
Overview
NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem built on Base Mainnet, combining digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse-style experiences under a unified brand. The ecosystem includes the NeoX Coin (NXC), curated NFT series, an HTML5 arcade game, and AI-enhanced multimedia projects.
History
NeoX originated as a creative digital art project and gradually expanded to incorporate blockchain technology, NFTs, and interactive media. In 2025, the NeoX Coin (NXC) was launched on Base Mainnet, marking NeoX’s transition into a broader on-chain ecosystem spanning collectibles, gaming, and metaverse experimentation.
NeoX Coin (NXC)
NXC is the utility token of the NeoX ecosystem and is deployed on Base Mainnet. It is designed to support participation across NeoX experiences and future metaverse features.

NeoX Arcade Game
NeoX includes a custom-built HTML5 arcade experience featuring multi-level progression, animated coin collectibles, score tracking, mobile optimization, and integrated brand visuals.
Metaverse Exhibition
NeoX maintains a virtual exhibition using OnCyber, showcasing NFTs, 3D avatars, cinematic scenes, and ambient media environments.
Technology Stack
  • Base Mainnet (L2)
  • Thirdweb for contract management
  • IPFS / Lighthouse decentralized storage
  • HTML5 + Canvas game development
  • VRM / glTF metaverse avatar assets
  • Responsive design for mobile & iPad
Future Development
Planned expansions include new metaverse zones, AI-generated micro-films, advanced game mechanics, and deeper cross-platform integrations across web, mobile, and VR.
