NeoX Official Wiki

Overview

NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem built on Base Mainnet, connecting digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse creativity under one unified brand. It includes the NeoX Coin (NXC), curated NFT collections, browser-based games, and AI-powered multimedia projects.

History

NeoX began as an artistic and experimental digital project before expanding into blockchain technology. In 2025, the NeoX Coin (NXC) was launched on Base Mainnet, marking the start of a multi-directional ecosystem combining NFTs, entertainment, gamification, and metaverse interactivity.

NeoX Coin (NXC)

NXC serves as the utility token of the NeoX ecosystem and is deployed on Base Mainnet. It enhances user participation across digital experiences, future metaverse features, and ecosystem access.

NFT Collections

NeoX includes several artistic NFT series, each representing a chapter of the brand’s creative identity:

  • NeoX Timekeeper Edition – 8 limited NFTs themed around time and perception.
  • NeoX Van Gogh Visions – Reimagined artworks inspired by Van Gogh.
  • NeoX Abstract Realms – Conceptual abstract art series.
  • NeoCoin Genesis Edition (G1) – Foundational collection of NeoX digital art.

NeoX Catch Game

The NeoX Catch Game is an interactive HTML5 experience where players collect coins, avoid obstacles, and progress through multiple levels. Features include:

  • Multi-level gameplay
  • Opening animations
  • Leaderboard & Top 3 rankings
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Integration with NeoX visual identity

Metaverse Exhibition

NeoX maintains a virtual exhibition powered by OnCyber and 3D environments, displaying NFTs, immersive avatars, and AI-enhanced audio-visual experiences.

Technology

NeoX utilizes:
• Base Mainnet (L2)
• Thirdweb smart contract deployment
• IPFS/Lighthouse for storage
• VRM/glTF for metaverse avatars
• HTML5 & Canvas for gaming
• iOS-friendly responsive design

Future Development

Planned developments include new metaverse spaces, additional NFT series, AI-driven micro-films, expanded game mechanics, and deeper cross-platform integration.
