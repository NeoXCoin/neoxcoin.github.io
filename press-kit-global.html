01 · Brand Overview

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a next-generation digital utility asset deployed on Base Mainnet, designed to orchestrate Web3 experiences across native NFT initiatives, digital fine arts, browser gaming architectures, and metaverse virtual spaces.

Positioned strictly as a creative utility token rather than a speculative product, NXC powers the interactive mechanics within the NeoX ecosystem, binding creator collections, gameplay dynamics, and cross-metaverse representation under a highly coherent global brand identity.
