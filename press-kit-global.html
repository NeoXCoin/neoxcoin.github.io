01 · Brand Overview

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a next-generation digital asset on Base Mainnet, designed to unify Web3 experiences across NFTs, digital art, interactive games, and metaverse-style exhibitions.

Built as a creative utility token rather than a speculative product, NXC connects NeoX NFT collections, browser-based mini-games, virtual galleries, and future digital-ownership experiences under a single brand.

NeoX focuses on clarity, creativity, and long-term identity, ensuring a consistent visual and cultural presence across all platforms.

02 · Mission & Vision

Vision

To build an accessible Web3 ecosystem where art, gaming, digital identity, and community interact seamlessly through a unified brand.

Mission

  • Introduce a simple yet powerful token for multi-platform utility.
  • Empower digital creators with open, accessible tools.
  • Develop immersive artistic and gamified experiences.
  • Bring Web3 culture closer to everyday audiences.

03 · What NeoX Represents

NeoX blends premium digital branding, high-quality art, and utility-focused innovation.

Core symbolism:

  • Gold → value, authenticity, and trust.
  • NeoX Blue → technology, innovation, and the digital future.

This dual-tone identity forms the foundation for all NeoX visuals — from NFTs and logos to gameplay interfaces and metaverse exhibitions.

04 · Token Overview (NXC)

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 utility token deployed on Base Mainnet. It acts as the core asset powering NeoX products, unlocking gated digital content, future utilities, and ecosystem features.

  • Future usage in digital item ownership, in-game integrations, and marketplace flows.

Note: NXC is a utility-focused token and is not designed as an investment product.

07 · NFT Ecosystem

NeoX hosts several curated NFT collections, each designed with a consistent visual language and narrative theme.

  • NeoX Timekeeper Edition – 8 official NFTs on Base Mainnet.
  • NeoX Van Gogh Visions – classical-inspired art fused with a futuristic NeoX aesthetic.
  • NeoX Abstract Realms – experimental abstract digital artworks.
  • NeoX Genesis & Genesis Edition – early series that established the project’s visual identity.

Selected collections are available on OpenSea under the official NeoX profile.

08 · Gaming & Metaverse

NeoX is actively developing interactive Web3 experiences that connect art, gaming, and digital ownership:

  • NeoX Catch the Coins – an HTML5 mini-game featuring dynamic coin mechanics and scoring.
  • NeoX Metaverse Exhibition – virtual gallery experiences hosted via OnCyber.
