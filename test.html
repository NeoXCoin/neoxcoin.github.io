NeoX Coin (NXC) – Whitepaper

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a community-driven utility token on Base Mainnet that powers the NeoX ecosystem of NFTs, digital art, casual gaming, and metaverse-style exhibition spaces.

Version 1.2 · Last updated: 11 November 2025

1. Abstract

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 token deployed on Base Mainnet and designed as the core utility token for the NeoX ecosystem. Instead of focusing purely on speculation, NeoX emphasizes creativity, visual identity, and playful Web3 experiences that connect NFTs, interactive content, and virtual exhibitions.

This whitepaper introduces the motivation behind NeoX, the technical foundation of NXC, the token design, and how NXC is intended to be used within the broader NeoX metaverse.

2. Vision & Motivation

NeoX was born from a simple idea:
“Web3 does not have to be only about price charts – it can also be about art, stories, and playful experiences.”

From this, four core principles guide NeoX:

  • Art-first: Focus on content and aesthetics before speculation. In the NeoX universe, NFTs are part of a story and a mood, not just isolated assets.
  • Fair launch: No presale and no private sale. The goal is to reduce insider advantages and keep the starting point more balanced for the community.
  • Multi-format experiences: NeoX is not just a single collection, but a broader world that can include illustration, animation, music, games, and virtual exhibitions under one brand.
  • Low fees & accessibility: By building on Base Mainnet, NeoX benefits from lower transaction costs, combined with multi-language interfaces to welcome users from different regions into Web3.

3. Technology Stack

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 token deployed on Base Mainnet using standardized smart-contract implementations and deployment tooling provided by thirdweb.

  • Blockchain: Base Mainnet (Ethereum Layer 2)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Contract Address: 0x9C17fDa33CBEe4aE3768252Fd62156034F42B40B
  • Explorer (BaseScan): View NXC on BaseScan

By leveraging thirdweb’s widely used smart-contract framework, NeoX reduces technical risk and complexity while retaining standard security and configuration options.

4. Token Design

NXC is designed as a straightforward, transparent token:

  • No rebasing, reflection, or complex mechanics.
  • No presale or private sale – NXC follows a community-first launch approach.
  • Fixed supply: The token supply is set at deployment; no additional minting is currently intended.
  • Admin controls: Limited to standard configuration and security parameters available in thirdweb contracts.

For the most accurate technical details, the on-chain contract on BaseScan should be treated as the ultimate source of truth.

5. NXC Utility in the NeoX Ecosystem

NXC is envisioned as the “energy core” of the NeoX metaverse, with potential use-cases that may be introduced gradually over time:

  • NFT ecosystem: NXC may be used in the future for special mints, limited drops, collector rewards, or access passes across NeoX series such as NeoX Timekeeper Edition, NeoX Van Gogh Visions, and NeoX Abstract Realms.
  • Gamified experiences: NeoX-themed browser games, such as the “NeoX Catch the Coins” concept, may integrate NXC for cosmetic items, event modes, or score-based rewards.
  • Metaverse & exhibitions: In virtual galleries and 3D exhibition spaces (for example the NeoX OnCyber Exhibition Space), NXC could be used to unlock special rooms, interactive installations, or limited experiences.
  • Community events: NXC can serve as a reward or participation credential in creative contests, community challenges, raffles, or collaboration campaigns.

All utilities described above are directional. Actual integrations may vary based on technical feasibility and regulatory considerations, and will be announced via official channels.

6. Ecosystem Components

The NeoX ecosystem already consists of several creative components and will continue to evolve:

  • NFT collections: Curated series such as NeoX Timekeeper Edition, NeoX Van Gogh Visions, and NeoX Abstract Realms, available through the official project wallet on OpenSea: NeoX Project Wallet NFTs .
  • Interactive web experiences & mini-games: For example, NeoX Catch the Coins, which lets visitors “play their way” into understanding NeoX Coin and its universe.
  • Metaverse-style exhibition spaces: Virtual galleries such as the NeoX OnCyber Exhibition Space enable more immersive exploration of NeoX artwork.
  • Official project wallet: The primary wallet associated with NeoX NFTs and ecosystem assets is: 0x2B15d8f1D634a84ACb33Fc2519acE3d13bD72BD2.

7. High-Level Roadmap

The NeoX roadmap intentionally keeps creative flexibility. A simplified outline:

  • Phase 1 – Foundation & brand: Deploy NXC on Base, launch the first NFT series, and establish the official website and visual identity.
  • Phase 2 – Experiences & interaction: Expand NeoX-themed interactive content and games, and refine the overall web experience.
  • Phase 3 – Utility expansion: Gradually test and introduce scenarios where NXC is used for minting, rewards, or gated access to selected experiences.
  • Phase 4 – Community & collaborations: Collaborate with artists, designers, and Web3 projects that align with NeoX’s art-first philosophy.
