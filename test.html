NeoX Coin (NXC) – Whitepaper

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a community-driven utility token on Base Mainnet, powering the NeoX ecosystem of NFTs, digital art, casual games, and metaverse-style exhibitions.

Version 1.2 (English Edition) · Last updated: 11 November 2025

This English edition is an official translation of the NeoX Coin (NXC) Whitepaper (Traditional Chinese Version, November 2025).

1. Abstract

NeoX Coin (NXC) is deployed on Base Mainnet and designed as a utility-focused token rather than a purely speculative asset. Its purpose is to act as a common value layer across the NeoX ecosystem – connecting digital art, NFT collections, casual games, and immersive virtual exhibition spaces.

This whitepaper provides a concise overview of the NeoX vision, technical foundation, token design, and how NXC may be used across current and future NeoX experiences.

2. Vision & Philosophy

NeoX began with a simple idea:
“Web3 does not have to be only about price volatility – it can also be about art, stories, and playful experiences.”

Based on this idea, NeoX is built around a few core principles:

  • Art-first: Content and aesthetics come first. In the NeoX world, NFTs are treated as pieces of a story and a mood, not just isolated assets.
  • Fair launch: No presale and no private sale. The goal is to reduce insider advantages and keep the starting point more balanced for the community.
  • Multi-format experiences: NeoX is not just a single collection, but a broader world that can include illustration, animation, music, games, and virtual exhibitions.
  • Low fees & accessibility: By building on Base Mainnet, NeoX benefits from lower transaction costs, combined with multi-language interfaces to welcome users from different regions into Web3.

3. Technology Foundation

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 token deployed on Base Mainnet, with contract deployment and management performed via the thirdweb tooling suite.

  • Blockchain: Base Mainnet (Ethereum Layer 2)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Contract Address: 0x9C17fDa33CBEe4aE3768252Fd62156034F42B40B
  • Block Explorer: View NXC on BaseScan

thirdweb provides widely used, standardized smart contract templates. This helps reduce deployment risks and technical complexity, while maintaining essential security and consistency across the ecosystem.

4. Token Design

NXC is intentionally designed to be simple and transparent. It does not use complex mechanisms such as rebasing, reflection, or automatic redistribution. Its primary role is to function as a utility and participation token across the NeoX ecosystem.

  • Presale / Private Sale: None. NXC was launched as a community-oriented fair launch token.
  • Total Supply: Defined at deployment time. There are currently no plans for additional minting after launch.
  • Admin Controls: Limited to standard safety and configuration parameters provided by thirdweb. NeoX is not designed around heavy centralized control.

For technical details, the on-chain contract as displayed on BaseScan should be treated as the ultimate source of truth.

5. NXC Utility in the NeoX Ecosystem

NXC is envisioned as the “energy core” of the NeoX metaverse, with multiple potential use cases. The following utilities are planned directions; actual implementation will be phased in over time:

  • NFT ecosystem:
    For special mints, limited editions, collector rewards, or airdrop-style campaigns, NXC may be used as a payment token, discount condition, or proof of participation.
  • Gamified experiences:
    In NeoX-themed mini games (for example, NeoX Catch the Coins), NXC may be integrated to unlock cosmetic items, special modes, or leaderboard rewards.
  • Virtual exhibitions & interactive spaces:
    Within NeoX virtual galleries or 3D exhibition spaces (such as the NeoX OnCyber gallery), NXC could be used in the future for unlocking special rooms, interactive installations, or limited experiences.
  • Community activities:
    NXC can serve as a reward or participation key for creative contests, community missions, raffles, or collaborative art campaigns.

The utilities listed above are planned directions and may be adjusted based on technical feasibility and regulatory considerations. All actual implementations and timelines will be communicated via official channels.

7. Roadmap (High-Level)

The NeoX roadmap is deliberately flexible to preserve creative freedom. The following is a high-level outline rather than a rigid schedule:

  • Phase 1 – Foundation & Identity
    Deploy NXC on Base Mainnet, launch initial NFT collections, and establish the official website and visual identity.
  • Phase 2 – Experiences & Interaction
    Develop additional NeoX-themed interactive content and mini games, refining user flows and visual design.
  • Phase 3 – Utility Expansion
    Gradually test and roll out NXC-based utilities, such as NFT minting, reward mechanisms, or access-based passes for certain experiences.
  • Phase 4 – Community & Collaborations
    Collaborate with artists, designers, and other Web3 projects to expand the creative scope and reach of NeoX.
