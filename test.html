NeoX Coin (NXC) – Whitepaper

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a community-driven utility token on Base Mainnet that powers the NeoX ecosystem of NFTs, digital art, casual gaming, and metaverse-style exhibitions.

Version 1.2 · Last updated: 11 November 2025

1. Abstract

NeoX Coin (NXC) is designed as the core utility token of the NeoX ecosystem – a creative Web3 universe that blends digital art, curated NFT collections, lightweight games, and immersive exhibition spaces. Built on Base Mainnet, NeoX aims to make Web3 feel artistic, playful, and accessible instead of purely speculative.

This whitepaper provides an overview of the NeoX vision, underlying technology, token design, and current and planned utilities of NXC within the broader ecosystem.

2. Vision & Motivation

NeoX is built on a simple idea:
“Web3 should not only be about price charts – it can also be about art, stories, and experiences.”

The project focuses on a few core principles:

  • Art-first: Content and aesthetics come first. In the NeoX universe, NFTs are part of a story and a mood, not just isolated assets.
  • Fair launch: No presale and no private sale. The goal is to reduce insider advantages and keep the starting point more balanced for the community.
  • Multi-format experiences: NeoX is not just a single collection, but a broader world that can include illustration, animation, music, games, and virtual exhibitions.
  • Low fees & accessibility: By building on Base Mainnet, NeoX benefits from lower transaction costs, combined with multi-language interfaces to welcome users from different regions into Web3.

3. Technology Stack

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 token deployed on Base Mainnet using standardized smart contract implementations and deployment tooling provided by thirdweb.

  • Blockchain: Base Mainnet (Ethereum Layer 2)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Contract Address: 0x9C17fDa33CBEe4aE3768252Fd62156034F42B40B
  • Explorer (BaseScan): View NXC on BaseScan

By leveraging thirdweb’s widely used smart-contract framework, NeoX reduces technical risk and complexity while retaining standard security and configuration options.

4. Token Design

NXC is designed as a straightforward, transparent token:

  • No rebasing, reflection, or complex mechanics.
  • No presale or private sale – NXC follows a community-first launch approach.
  • Fixed supply: The token supply is set at deployment; no additional minting is currently intended.
  • Admin controls: Limited to standard configuration and security parameters available in thirdweb contracts.

For the most accurate technical details, the on-chain contract on BaseScan should be treated as the ultimate source of truth.

5. NXC Utility in the NeoX Ecosystem

NXC is envisioned as the “energy core” of the NeoX ecosystem. Over time, it may be used in a variety of ways, rolled out progressively:

  • NFT ecosystem: NXC may be used for special mint events, limited editions, collector rewards, or access passes across different NeoX collections.
  • Gamified experiences: In NeoX-themed mini-games such as NeoX Catch the Coins, NXC could be used for unlocking cosmetics, special modes, or event rewards.
  • Virtual exhibitions & interaction: In NeoX virtual galleries or 3D spaces – for example the NeoX OnCyber exhibition space – NXC may be integrated for special rooms, interactive installations, or limited experiences.
  • Community events: NXC can be used for creative contests, quests, raffles, or collaborative art initiatives.

All utility examples above are directional and may be implemented gradually, subject to technical and regulatory considerations. Concrete features will always be announced through official channels.

7. High-Level Roadmap

The NeoX roadmap keeps room for creative flexibility. A high-level outline:

  • Phase 1 – Foundation & branding
    Deploy NXC on Base, launch initial NFT collections, and establish the official website and visual identity.
  • Phase 2 – Experiences & interaction
    Expand NeoX-themed interactive content and mini-games; improve navigation and visuals across web experiences.
  • Phase 3 – Deeper utility
    Experiment with integrating NXC into NFT minting, reward systems, and event access mechanisms.
  • Phase 4 – Community & collaborations
    Collaborate with artists, designers, and Web3 projects that align with NeoX’s art-first philosophy.
