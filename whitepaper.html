NeoX Coin (NXC) – Whitepaper

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a community-driven token on Base Mainnet that powers the NeoX ecosystem of NFTs, digital art, and immersive Web3 experiences.

Document Version: 1.0  |  Last updated: 10 November 2025

1. Abstract

NeoX Coin (NXC) is designed as the core utility token of the NeoX ecosystem – a creative Web3 universe that combines digital art, NFT collections, casual gaming, and metaverse-style exhibition spaces. Built on Base Mainnet, NeoX aims to provide an accessible, low-fee environment for users to collect, interact with, and participate in curated digital experiences.

2. Vision & Motivation

The NeoX project was created with a simple idea: Web3 should feel artistic, playful, and human – not only financial. NeoX focuses on storytelling through NFTs, interactive mini-games, and immersive galleries, while using NXC as the connective layer for value and participation.

  • Art-first: Highlight digital art and curation instead of pure speculation.
  • Fair access: Launch without presale or private sale, reducing early insider advantages.
  • Multi-format experiences: Support static art, animation, music, and simple games under one brand.
  • Low fees: Leverage Base Mainnet for cost-effective transactions and accessibility.

3. Technology Stack

  • Blockchain: Base Mainnet (an Ethereum Layer 2 network)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Contract Address: 0x9C17fDa33CBEe4aE3768252Fd62156034F42B40B
  • Explorer (BaseScan): View on BaseScan

4. Token Design

  • Presale / Private Sale: None – fair community launch.
  • Minting after deployment: Not intended; supply is fixed at deployment level.
  • Admin controls: Limited to standard configuration and security parameters provided by thirdweb.

5. Utility within the NeoX Ecosystem

  • NFT Ecosystem: Used for minting, discounts, or gated NFT drops across NeoX collections.
  • Games: Integrated into NeoX’s “Catch the Coins” and future GameFi prototypes for rewards and items.
  • Metaverse: Used in NeoX OnCyber galleries for featured placement or interactive experiences.
  • Community Events: Campaigns, raffles, and art challenges powered by NXC.

6. Ecosystem Components

  • NFT Collections: Curated series such as NeoX Timekeeper Edition, Van Gogh Visions, and Abstract Realms on OpenSea.
  • Interactive Game Portal: NeoX Catch the Coins.
  • Metaverse Exhibition: NeoX Exhibition on OnCyber.
  • Official Project Wallet: 0x2B15d8f1D634a84ACb33Fc2519acE3d13bD72BD2.

7. Roadmap

  • Phase 1 – Foundation: Launch token, website, and first NFT series.
  • Phase 2 – Experiences: Add mini-games and interactive content.
  • Phase 3 – Utility Expansion: Integrate NXC across NFTs and games.
  • Phase 4 – Community & Partnerships: Collaborate with creators and partners.
