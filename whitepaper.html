NeoX Coin (NXC) – Whitepaper

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a community-driven token on Base Mainnet that powers the NeoX ecosystem of NFTs, digital art, and immersive Web3 experiences.

Version 1.0  |  Last updated: November 2025

1. Abstract

NeoX Coin (NXC) is designed as the core utility token of the NeoX ecosystem – a creative Web3 universe that combines digital art, NFT collections, casual gaming, and metaverse-style exhibition spaces. Built on Base Mainnet, NeoX aims to provide an accessible, low-fee environment for users to collect, interact with, and participate in curated digital experiences.

This whitepaper provides an overview of the motivation, design principles, and usage of NXC within the NeoX ecosystem.

2. Vision & Motivation

The NeoX project was created with a simple idea: Web3 should feel artistic, playful, and human – not only financial. NeoX focuses on storytelling through NFTs, interactive mini-games, and immersive galleries, while using NXC as the connective layer for value and participation.

  • Art-first: Highlight digital art and curation instead of pure speculation.
  • Fair access: Launch without presale or private sale, reducing early insider advantages.
  • Multi-format experiences: Support static art, animation, music, and simple games under one brand.
  • Low fees: Leverage Base Mainnet for cost-effective transactions and accessibility.

3. Technology Stack

NeoX Coin (NXC) is an ERC-20 compliant token deployed on Base Mainnet, using audited infrastructure and standard token patterns provided via thirdweb.

  • Blockchain: Base Mainnet (an Ethereum Layer 2 network)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20
  • Contract Address: 0x9C17fDa33CBEe4aE3768252Fd62156034F42B40B
  • Explorer (BaseScan): View on BaseScan

Smart contract deployment and configuration were performed through the thirdweb tooling suite, which provides standardized implementations and contract management.

4. Token Design

NXC is intended to be a straightforward, transparent token with no complex rebasing or reflection mechanisms. It is designed primarily for utility within the NeoX ecosystem rather than as a high-risk financial instrument.

  • Presale / Private Sale: None – fair community launch.
  • Minting after deployment: Not intended; supply is fixed at deployment level.
  • Admin controls: Limited to standard configuration and security parameters provided by thirdweb.

For the most accurate technical details, the on-chain contract on BaseScan should be considered the single source of truth.

5. NXC Utility within the NeoX Ecosystem

NXC is envisioned as the central utility token for various current and future NeoX experiences:

  • NFT Ecosystem: NXC may be used in the future for minting, discounts, or access to limited drops across collections such as: NeoX Timekeeper Edition, Van Gogh Visions, Abstract Realms, and other series.
  • Gamified Experiences: Simple Web3-friendly games (e.g., “NeoX Catch the Coins”) can integrate NXC for cosmetic items, score-based rewards, or event participation, subject to future development.
  • Metaverse & Exhibitions: NeoX virtual exhibition spaces may use NXC for special access, featured placement, or interactive experiences.
  • Community & Events: NXC can be used in community campaigns such as challenges, raffles, or collaborative art events.

Note: Actual integrations may roll out gradually. All utilities will be announced via official channels.

6. Ecosystem Components

The NeoX ecosystem currently includes, but is not limited to:

  • NFT Collections: Curated series such as NeoX Timekeeper Edition, NeoX Van Gogh Visions, NeoX Abstract Realms, and more.
  • Interactive Web Experiences: Browser-based mini-games and interactive landing pages themed around NeoX Coin and its universe.
  • Metaverse-style Exhibitions: 3D or immersive galleries where visitors can explore NeoX artworks and potentially interact with NXC-based features.
  • Official Project Wallet: NFT and ecosystem assets are primarily managed through the project wallet: 0x2B15d8f1D634a84ACb33Fc2519acE3d13bD72BD2.

7. Roadmap (High-Level)

The NeoX roadmap is intentionally flexible and creative-first. A simplified outline:

  • Phase 1 – Foundation: Deploy NXC on Base, launch initial NFT collections, establish official website and branding.
  • Phase 2 – Experiences: Expand interactive web experiences, refine NeoX game concepts, and strengthen the visual identity of NeoX.
  • Phase 3 – Deeper Utility: Explore integrating NXC with selected NFT drops, game rewards, or gated experiences, subject to technical and regulatory considerations.
  • Phase 4 – Community & Partnerships: Collaborate with artists, creators, and communities that align with NeoX’s art-first philosophy.
