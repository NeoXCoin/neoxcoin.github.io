NeoX (NXC) – Ecosystem Overview

A reference-style summary of the NeoX ecosystem on Base Mainnet, including the NXC utility token, NFT collections, browser-based game experiences, metaverse exhibition spaces, and planned future development.

Base Mainnet ERC-20 Token NFT Collections Game Metaverse

Overview

NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem built on Base Mainnet, combining digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse-style interactive experiences under a unified brand. The ecosystem includes the NeoX Coin (NXC) utility token, curated NFT series, an HTML5 arcade-style game, and AI-enhanced multimedia projects.

History

NeoX originally began as a creative digital art project and gradually expanded to integrate blockchain technology, NFTs, and interactive media. Over time, the project evolved from one-off artworks into a more coherent on-chain universe, experimenting with collectibles, gaming, and metaverse scenes.

In 2025, the NeoX Coin (NXC) was officially launched on Base Mainnet as the utility token of the ecosystem, marking NeoX’s transition into a broader Web3 environment spanning NFTs, game experiences, and virtual exhibition spaces.

NeoX Coin (NXC)

NXC is the utility token of the NeoX ecosystem and is deployed on Base Mainnet (L2). It is designed to support participation across current and future NeoX experiences, with potential roles in collectibles, in-game utilities, and metaverse interactions.

NFT Collections

NeoX features multiple curated NFT series, each exploring different visual themes and concepts. Collections include:

  • NeoCoin Genesis Edition (G1) – early symbolic pieces tied to the origin of NeoX.
  • NeoX: Timekeeper Edition – a limited series themed around time, rhythm, and motion.
  • NeoX Van Gogh Visions – art inspired by classical painting aesthetics with a NeoX twist.
  • NeoX Abstract Realms – experimental, abstract works exploring color and form.

These NFTs act as both art collectibles and reference points for future experiences in the NeoX universe, including potential in-game representation, gallery showcases, and metaverse displays.

Game – NeoX Catch (HTML5)

NeoX Catch is a browser-based HTML5 arcade-style game where players catch NeoX coins, avoid obstacles, and aim for high scores across multiple stages. The game is optimized for mobile devices and can be played directly from the NeoX website without installation.

Key features include:

  • Multi-level design with opening and end scenes.
  • Score tracking, including a Top 3 leaderboard display.
  • Mobile-first responsive layout for phones and tablets.
  • Visual integration with the NeoX brand identity and coin designs.

Metaverse Exhibition

NeoX maintains virtual exhibition environments using platforms such as OnCyber, featuring NFT artworks, 3D avatar integration, ambient music, and cinematic scenes. These spaces are used to present NeoX collections in immersive layouts and to experiment with gallery-style storytelling.

As new NFT series and visual concepts are introduced, the exhibition spaces are periodically updated with new rooms, scenes, and curated experiences.

Technology Stack

NeoX makes use of the following key technologies:

  • Base Mainnet (L2) for token deployment and on-chain interactions.
  • Thirdweb for smart-contract deployment and management.
  • IPFS / Lighthouse for decentralized media and metadata storage.
  • HTML5 & Canvas for the browser-based NeoX Catch game.
  • VRM / glTF assets for avatars and metaverse-ready 3D visuals.
  • Responsive web design optimized for both mobile and desktop devices.

Future Development

Planned development for the NeoX ecosystem includes:

  • Expanding metaverse exhibition spaces and interactive environments.
  • Launching new NFT collections and experimental visual series.
  • AI-assisted micro-films, narrative pieces, and cinematic projects under the NeoX brand.
  • Further upgrades to game mechanics, progression systems, and visual FX.
  • Deeper cross-platform integration across Web, mobile, and virtual-world environments.

The goal is to gradually connect art, gaming, and metaverse scenes into a cohesive, long-term Web3 ecosystem powered by NXC on Base Mainnet.
