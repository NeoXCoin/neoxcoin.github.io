NeoX (NXC) – Ecosystem Overview

This official reference guide details the NeoX ecosystem on Base Mainnet, encompassing the NXC utility token, custom NFT collections, HTML5 arcade gaming, immersive metaverse exhibitions, and future developments.

Project Overview

NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem deployed on Base Mainnet that converges digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse interactive experiences under a unified brand. The ecosystem consists of the NeoX Coin (NXC) utility token, curated NFT lines, HTML5-based web arcade games, and AI-assisted multimedia content.

History

NeoX originated as a creative digital art project, gradually incorporating blockchain technology, NFTs, and interactive media. Over time, the initiative transitioned from isolated artistic releases into a coherent on-chain universe, exploring collectibles, gaming, and metaverse environments.

In 2025, the official deployment of NeoX Coin (NXC) on Base Mainnet marked the formal evolution of NeoX into a fully tokenized Web3 ecosystem, linking digital collections, gaming utilities, and virtual spaces.

NeoX Coin (NXC)

NXC is the primary utility token of the NeoX ecosystem, built natively on Base Mainnet (L2). It is engineered to facilitate user participation across current and upcoming NeoX products, playing an active role in collectibles, in-game assets, and virtual world interactions.

NFT Collections

NeoX has launched several themed NFT collections, each exploring distinct visual styles and conceptual designs. Core collections include:

  • NeoCoin Genesis Edition (G1) – The earliest symbolic art pieces honoring the origin of NeoX.
  • NeoX: Timekeeper Edition – A limited series exploring time, rhythm, and dynamic aesthetics.
  • NeoX Van Gogh Visions – A tribute collection merging classic painterly styles with futuristic tech motifs.
  • NeoX Abstract Realms – An experimental release emphasizing geometry, fluid color arrangements, and structural form.

These NFTs act as digital art collectibles and double as credentials for future ecosystem layers, including virtual galleries, token-gated access, and upcoming metaverse developments.

Arcade Gaming – NeoX Catch (HTML5)

NeoX Catch is a browser-based HTML5 arcade game where players navigate levels to collect NeoX coins, avoid obstacles, and aim for high scores on a competitive leaderboard. The experience is fully optimized for cross-platform play, requiring no downloads.

Key Features:

  • Multi-level structure complete with cinematic opening and ending cutscenes.
  • Real-time score tracking with an elite Top 3 leaderboard.
  • Mobile-first responsive layouts for smooth execution on smartphones and tablets.
  • Visuals and coins directly themed after the official NeoX ecosystem design.

Metaverse Exhibitions

NeoX utilizes advanced virtual architectures (such as OnCyber) to create 3D exhibition spaces that combine digital art, custom Avatars, background audio, and cinematic camera tracking. These spaces provide an immersive gallery experience for community storytelling and NFT showcases.

As new collections emerge, these virtual spaces undergo thematic updates, expanding into multi-room environments and interactive community hangouts.

Technology Stack

The NeoX infrastructure relies on a modern, decentralized stack:

  • Base Mainnet (L2) – For secure, fast, and cost-effective on-chain asset processing.
  • Thirdweb – For streamlined deployment, testing, and management of ecosystem smart contracts.
  • IPFS / Lighthouse – For decentralized, permanent storage of multi-media artwork and metadata.
  • HTML5 & Canvas – Powering the instantaneous, browser-native gameplay of NeoX Catch.
  • VRM / glTF 3D Assets – Enabling standard-compliant, platform-agnostic 3D virtual objects and avatars.
  • Responsive Web Architecture – Ensuring fluid navigation across desktop monitors and mobile touchscreens.

Future Roadmap

The upcoming milestones for the expansion of the NeoX universe include:

  • Expanding metaverse properties with multiplayer elements and larger exhibition hubs.
  • Introducing new thematic NFT drops exploring experimental digital aesthetics.
  • Developing AI-assisted lore videos, micro-movies, and narrative shorts under the NeoX brand.
  • Upgrading browser game core mechanics with persistent cloud saves and enhanced special effects (FX).
  • Deepening cross-chain/cross-platform asset utility across diverse digital landscapes.

Our long-term objective is to form a highly integrated, sustainable Web3 environment where art, casual gaming, and virtual venues enrich each other, powered by the utility of NXC on Base Mainnet.
