Overview

NeoX is a decentralized Web3 ecosystem built on Base Mainnet, combining digital art, blockchain gaming, and metaverse-style interactive experiences under a unified brand. The ecosystem includes the NeoX Coin (NXC) utility token, curated NFT series, an HTML5 arcade-style game, and AI-enhanced multimedia projects.
