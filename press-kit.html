About NeoX Coin (NXC)

NeoX Coin (NXC) is a Web3 art and metaverse token built on Base Mainnet. It powers a growing creative ecosystem that connects digital art, interactive games and virtual exhibitions under one unified brand: NeoX.

Rather than focusing purely on speculation, NeoX Coin aims to become the creative fuel for artists, collectors, gamers and Web3 explorers who want to experience new forms of art, storytelling and interaction on-chain.

Ecosystem Overview

NeoX is a long-term creative project exploring how art, storytelling, music and interactive experiences can live together on-chain.

The ecosystem includes:

  • Original NFT collections with thematic storytelling
  • Browser-based mini games set in the NeoX universe
  • Virtual exhibition spaces in metaverse-style galleries
  • Future AI-assisted films, music projects and 3D worlds

NeoX Coin (NXC) is the central token that links these creative layers together.

Token Utility (Current & Future)

NeoX Coin is designed as a creative utility token within the NeoX ecosystem:

  • Ecosystem Access – entry to certain experiences, events or gated content.
  • Community Reward Experiments – potential rewards for active supporters and participants.
  • Creative Mechanics – used in art drops, interactive installations and narrative experiments.
  • Metaverse & Game Integration – soft currency or access token for special features over time.

NeoX Coin is not marketed as a financial product and does not guarantee returns or price performance. All utilities are implemented gradually and transparently as the project evolves.
