Frequently Asked Questions
Is NeoX a scam?
Absolutely not. NeoX is a transparent, educational cryptocurrency initiative designed to explore Web3 technology through NFTs and utility tokens. All smart contracts are verifiable on blockchain, and our project is open-source on GitHub. We do not solicit private investments, promise returns, or engage in deceptive marketing. NeoX is a self-funded, public Web3 experiment created for learning, inspiration, and innovation.
How can I get NeoX tokens?
NeoX tokens (NXC) can be obtained through our official smart contract via MetaMask or Thirdweb. We also provide step-by-step guides on how to purchase NXC in our “How to Buy” section.
Is there a whitelist or presale?
No. Our initial test versions are open to everyone. When public sales are available, the process will be transparent and accessible through our official channels only.
Is NeoX officially registered as a company?
NeoX is currently a community-driven initiative, not a registered legal entity. Our goal is to remain open, flexible, and exploratory while maintaining transparency and ethical standards.
Where can I view NeoX's smart contracts?
You can view and verify all deployed smart contracts on block explorers such as Sepolia Etherscan. Contract addresses are published on our website for public verification.
How can I stay up to date?
Follow our official Instagram at @neoxofficial for updates, educational content, and roadmap information.
