🛒 How to Buy NeoX

  1. Install the MetaMask browser extension or mobile app.
  2. Open MetaMask and switch your network to Sepolia Testnet.
  3. Claim test ETH using a faucet:
    • 🔗 Recommended: Google Sepolia Faucet (Web3 supported)
    • Alternative: sepoliafaucet.com
  4. Visit the NeoX website and click “Add to MetaMask” to add the NXC token.
  5. Click the “Buy” or “Mint” button to receive NeoX tokens.
  6. Check your NXC balance in MetaMask once the transaction is complete.
