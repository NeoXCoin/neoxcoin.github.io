Step 1: Install MetaMask

Download the MetaMask wallet from the official website or mobile app store.

Step 2: Connect to Sepolia Testnet

Open MetaMask and switch the network to Sepolia. If you don’t see it, go to “Settings → Advanced → Show test networks”.
