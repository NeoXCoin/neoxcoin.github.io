How to Buy NeoX

  1. Connect your wallet (e.g. MetaMask) and switch to the Sepolia Testnet.
  2. Get test ETH: Make sure your wallet has test ETH. You can get free test ETH from a faucet.
  3. Visit the NeoX Token Page on Thirdweb:
    NeoX Token on Thirdweb
  4. Click “Buy” and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.
  5. (Optional) Add the NXC token to MetaMask using the button provided on the page.
