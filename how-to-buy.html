How to Buy NeoX

  1. Install MetaMask and connect it to your browser.
  2. Switch the network to Sepolia Testnet.
  3. Claim Sepolia ETH from a faucet (e.g., sepoliafaucet.com).
  4. Visit the NeoX website and click “Add NeoX to MetaMask”.
  5. Click the “Mint” button to receive test NeoX Token.
  6. Check your MetaMask wallet to verify your balance.
