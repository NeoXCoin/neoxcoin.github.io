How to Buy NeoX

  1. Connect your wallet (e.g. MetaMask) and switch to the Sepolia Testnet.
  2. Make sure your wallet has test ETH. You can get free test ETH from a faucet.
  3. Visit the official NeoX Token page on Thirdweb.
  4. Click “Buy” and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.
  5. (Optional) Add the NXC token to MetaMask using the button provided below.
